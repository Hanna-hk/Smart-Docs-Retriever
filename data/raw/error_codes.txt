DIAGNOSTIC TROUBLE CODES (DTC) - HPU-2000

E-101: Low Oil Level
Description: Reservoir fluid level has dropped below the critical threshold.
Possible Causes: External leakage, seal failure, or insufficient filling during maintenance.
Action: Inspect system for puddles. Refill with ISO VG 46 oil immediately.

E-205: Filter Clog Detected
Description: Differential pressure across the return filter exceeds 3.5 bar.
Possible Causes: Contaminated fluid or overdue filter replacement.
Action: Replace filter element (Part No. F-992). Check oil cleanliness.

E-300: Pump Cavitation Warning
Description: System detects acoustic anomalies consistent with air bubbles in the fluid.
Possible Causes: Suction strainer blocked, low oil level, or air intake in the suction line.
Action: Check suction line tightness. Ensure the suction valve is fully open.

E-501: Thermal Overload / Overheating
Description: Fluid temperature sensor reads above 85Â°C.
Possible Causes: Cooler fan failure, heat exchanger blockage, or excessive duty cycle.
Action: Reduce system load. Check the water-cooling flow or clean the air-cooler fins.

E-999: Emergency Stop Activated
Description: The physical E-Stop button was pressed.
Action: Release the button and reset the safety relay to resume operation.
